What is Alkaline Water?

Water is a combination of hydrogen and oxygen. Thats why we call it H20. The amount of hydrogen in water is measured as pH levels. Alkaline water has more hydrogen -- or a higher pH -- than regular water.


Water's pH level ranges on a scale from 0 to 14. Seven is considered neutral or balanced between acidic and alkaline. If water is below 7 on the pH scale, it's "acidic." If it's higher than 7, it's "alkaline." D Sakura Alkaline Water has a pH level of 8.0+. That's why you should choose alkaline water over regular water.
